📜 The Challenge

A stone door blocks your path with three inscriptions:

  1. "One who persists never fails."
  2. "One who helps others grows twice."
  3. "One who learns always levels up."

Under the door a slit holds three tokens: Practice, Patience, Perseverance.

Options:

  • 💠 Insert Practice — Door opens
  • ❌ Try to force the door (danger)
  • ↩️ Abandon challenge and return
